The 2019 Porsche GT3 RS 991.2 is a high-performance sports car known for its track-focused design and exceptional driving dynamics. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine producing 520 horsepower and 346 lb-ft of torque. With a lightweight construction and precise handling, it delivers exhilarating performance on both road and track.

The 2018 Lamborghini Aventador SVJ Coupe is a flagship supercar renowned for its striking design and blistering performance. Equipped with a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine, it generates a whopping 770 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. With advanced aerodynamics and cutting-edge technology, the SVJ Coupe offers extraordinary speed and agility, making it one of the most sought-after hypercars on the market.
